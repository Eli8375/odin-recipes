Eggs

Some scrambled eggs

All one needs to make a serving of scrambled eggs are some butter, salt and pepper, milk and eggs.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a a bowl
  2. Spread butter onto a medium-heated pan
  3. Pour milk into bowl with eggs and whisk
  4. Add some salt and pepper
  5. Pour into pan and whisk as it heats up
